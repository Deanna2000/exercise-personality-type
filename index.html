ENFJ-A: The Protagonist

Five Traits That Are Most Like Me:

  • 1

    Collaboration:
    Pride and joy in guiding others to work together to improve themselves and their community.

  • 2

    Envisioning:
    Bring disconnected ideas and events together and communicate them as a common goal.

  • 3

    Empathizing:
    Seeing other people’s problems in themselves, trying to fix something in themselves that isn’t wrong.

  • 4

    Parenting:
    As parents, they will encourage their children to explore and grow, recognizing.

  • 5

    Buy-In:
    The end goal is always to get done what they set out to do in a way that leaves everyone involved satisfied.
